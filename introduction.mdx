Vista brings cutting-edge camera and display technology to Minecraft. Create security systems, build home theaters, or broadcast live feeds across your world with multi-block TVs, rotatable viewfinders, and a complete video cassette system.

## What Makes Vista Special?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Multi-Loader Support">
    Vista works on both **Fabric** and **NeoForge** mod loaders, giving you flexibility in your modpack setup. Built with the Architectury framework for seamless cross-platform compatibility.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Advanced Rendering">
    Features custom shaders, MSAA anti-aliasing, CRT overlay effects, and adaptive update scheduling for optimal performance. Vista renders camera feeds with the same quality as your main view.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Extensive Integrations">
    Works with popular mods including ComputerCraft (for programmable cameras), Supplementaries, Distant Horizons, Exposure, and more. Vista enhances your existing mod ecosystem.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Data-Driven Content">
    Create custom cassette tapes through JSON files. Define colors, sounds, and video content without writing code. Perfect for map makers and modpack creators.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Use Cases

Vista enables countless creative possibilities in Minecraft:

- **Security Systems**: Monitor your base with viewfinders connected to a central TV room
- **Home Theaters**: Build cinema rooms with massive multi-block screens
- **Museums**: Display video content on TVs throughout your exhibits
- **Adventure Maps**: Create cinematics and cutscenes with cassette tapes
- **Redstone Projects**: Use comparator output for smart automation
- **ComputerCraft Integration**: Program automated camera systems
