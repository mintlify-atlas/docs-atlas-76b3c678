This quickstart guide will walk you through creating your first Vista television and viewfinder system, from crafting to broadcasting.

## What You'll Build

By the end of this guide, you'll have:
- A working television display
- A camera viewfinder
- A live broadcast feed connecting them

<Note>
  Make sure you have Vista and Moonlight Lib installed. See [Installation](/installation) for setup instructions.
</Note>

## Step 1: Craft Your Blocks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Craft a Television">
    You'll need:
    - 4 Tinted Glass Panes
    - 1 Diamond
    - 2 Redstone Dust
    - 2 Any Planks

    Place them in a crafting table to create your television block.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Craft a Viewfinder">
    You'll need:
    - 1 Spyglass
    - 4 Iron Ingots
    - 4 Any Planks

    Combine these in a crafting table to create your viewfinder camera.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Craft a Hollow Cassette">
    You'll need:
    - 1 Regular Cassette (found in loot chests or from creeper drops)
    - 1 Echo Shard

    This special cassette will link your viewfinder to your TV for live broadcasts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Place and Test Your TV

<Steps>
  <Step title="Place the Television">
    Place your television block facing the direction you want. TVs can be placed on any surface.

    <Tip>
      Place multiple TVs next to each other - they'll automatically connect to form a larger screen!
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Power Your TV">
    TVs respond to redstone power:
    - Place a lever or redstone torch next to the TV
    - Activate it to turn on the TV
    - The TV will emit light level 3 when powered on
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test with a Cassette">
    If you have a regular cassette tape:
    1. Right-click the TV while holding the cassette
    2. The cassette will be inserted and start playing
    3. Right-click again to pause/resume
    4. Sneak + right-click to eject the cassette
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Set Up Your Camera

<Steps>
  <Step title="Place the Viewfinder">
    Place your viewfinder block on a wall, floor, or ceiling. The camera will point away from the surface it's attached to.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Mount the Camera">
    Right-click the viewfinder to mount it:
    - Your player will enter camera mode
    - Use your mouse to look around
    - Scroll to zoom (up to 44x zoom!)
    - Press your sneak key to dismount
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test Camera Controls">
    While mounted on the viewfinder:
    - **Look Around**: Move your mouse to pan and tilt
    - **Zoom In/Out**: Use scroll wheel
    - **Lock View**: Right-click to lock/unlock the camera angle
    - **Dismount**: Press your sneak key (default: Shift)

    The camera has full 360° rotation and can look in any direction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Create a Live Broadcast

<Steps>
  <Step title="Link the Hollow Cassette">
    1. Hold your hollow cassette
    2. Right-click on the **viewfinder** you want to broadcast from
    3. You'll see a message confirming the link
    4. The hollow cassette now contains a live feed from that camera
  </Step>

  <Step title="Insert Into TV">
    1. Right-click your TV while holding the linked hollow cassette
    2. The TV will now display the live camera feed
    3. The feed updates in real-time as you or others move the viewfinder
  </Step>

  <Step title="Watch Your Broadcast">
    - Position your viewfinder to point at an area you want to monitor
    - The TV will show exactly what the viewfinder sees
    - You can have multiple TVs showing the same feed
    - Create multiple hollow cassettes for different cameras

    <Info>
      The broadcast works across any distance and even across dimensions!
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="TV stays off when powered">
    - Make sure you have a redstone signal reaching the TV
    - Check that the TV block isn't obstructed
    - Try breaking and replacing the TV block
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Camera feed is black">
    - Make sure the viewfinder is in a loaded chunk
    - Check that there's a valid hollow cassette in the TV
    - Verify the hollow cassette is linked to a viewfinder
    - Try re-linking the hollow cassette
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't mount the viewfinder">
    - Make sure you're not in creative mode spectator
    - Check that you're right-clicking the correct block
    - Verify you're not already mounted on another viewfinder
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="TVs won't connect into larger displays">
    - TVs must face the same direction to connect
    - Check `max_connected_tv_size` in config (default: 8)
    - Try placing TVs without sneaking
    - Remove any blocks between TVs
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
